Jot

Where https://liamhtml.github.io/jot
About Jot was a small project I created while procrastinating doing a school assignment. I wanted a place to conveniently write down what I had to do, and thus jot was born. It has the simple premise of essentially being a notepad which you can easily close.
Details
  • The jot textarea can be resized to fit your needs!
  • You can easily display your browser's annoying spellcheck feature by checking the "disable spellcheck" checkbox.
